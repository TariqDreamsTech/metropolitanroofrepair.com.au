Our Leak Detection Process

We trace the entry point with roof inspection, moisture meters and controlled hose tests. You’ll get photos and a clear, fixed quote.

We stop water immediately (tarp/temporary seal), then complete the permanent repair—flashings, valleys/box gutters, skylights, cracked/slipped tiles, or targeted re-pointing.
